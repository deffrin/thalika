Corporate Philosophy

Social and environmental responsibilities are the core parts of Thalika’s culture and business. We are committed to nourishing people by providing the purest quality product and reducing the negative impact on the planet. From sourcing 100% natural products to obtaining fair trade certification, to greening our facilities and operations, we are constantly seeking ways to better protect our environment and ensure a better quality life for the communities we serve.

MISSION

Our mission at Thalika is to bring health and well-being to everything we touch. To discover, develop, innovate and successfully reach the community with the best pure biodegradable plates.

In our journey, we ensure to deliver the best value proposition as per global standards in each and every area of the total satisfaction of our customers, thereby contributing to the nation’s growth.

VISION

We see an enhanced lifestyle where diseases and ailments succumb to more enlightened, balanced, and holistic therapeutic management practices in harmony with mind, body, and soul.

We see a world where natural habitats of flora and fauna are nurtured, replenished and sustained organically. We see a world where fair trade and community advocacy flourish in conjunction with pursuit of economic wealth.

Zero Waste

Since Thalika plates are 100% biodegradable, they are completely earth-friendly and do not leave behind any toxic chemicals or residue. Moreover, they have an advantage over other plates that are only biodegradable since when they degrade, they release nutrients into the soil.

Moreover, at Thalika, the water used for cleaning and manufacturing waste is collected and is used to make food for animals. Thalika plates can certainly make cleaning up after a party easy and convenient. Thalika also collects the used plates after parties and recycle it to make maneuver for plants.
