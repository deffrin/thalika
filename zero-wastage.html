Zero Wastage

Since Thalika plates are 100% biodegradable, they are completely earth-friendly and do not leave behind any toxic chemicals or residue. Moreover, they have an advantage over other plates that are only biodegradable since when they degrade, they release nutrients into the soil. Moreover, at Thalika, the water used for cleaning and manufacturing waste is collected and is used to make food for animals.

Thalika plates can certainly make cleaning up after a party easy and convenient. Thalika also collects the used plates after parties and recycle it to make maneuver for plants.
